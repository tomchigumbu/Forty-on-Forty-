Welcome

A Peaceful Retreat in
the Heart of Burnside

Nestled in the leafy, sought-after suburb of Burnside, 44 on Burnside is a beautifully appointed, fully self-catering home offering everything you need for a comfortable and memorable stay in Bulawayo.

Whether you are visiting on business, exploring Zimbabwe’s second city, or simply looking for a relaxing getaway, this entire property is yours to enjoy — private, secure, and fully equipped from arrival to departure.

Wake up to birdsong in a quiet residential neighbourhood, just minutes from Burnside’s shops, restaurants, and Bulawayo’s key attractions. Everything about 44 on Burnside is designed to feel like home.
